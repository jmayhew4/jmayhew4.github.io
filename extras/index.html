Labyrinth


Unity3D / C#

Take control of a master of the elements, blast away enemies, and rack up a high score!

A 2D combat-platformeing game focused on defeating enemies and traversing a maze-like environment. The goal of the game is to kill the enemies in the maze, whom are assigned one element, and each enemy can only be killed by the element they are weak to.
